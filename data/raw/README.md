# EcoTwin-FM Raw Data Folder

Place problem-statement datasets here.

## Required / Useful Files

- `uci_air_quality/AirQualityUCI.csv` - already downloaded.
- `weatherbench/` - WeatherBench/ERA5 CSV, NetCDF, or Zarr files.
- `cities/india_cities.csv` - 30 Indian city metadata file.
- `energy/energy_demand.csv` - optional real city energy demand data.
- `urban_heat/urban_temperature.csv` - optional city surface temperature or urban heat data.

Template files are included for weather, energy, and urban heat.
